# Data Dictionary

## trade_date
Publication date of the Sayrafa exchange rate.

## sayrafa_avg_rate_lbp
Average Sayrafa exchange rate published by Banque du Liban, denominated in Lebanese Pounds (LBP) per USD.

## parallel_market_avg_rate_lbp
Estimated average parallel-market USD/LBP exchange rate for the corresponding date.

## trading_volume_usd
Reported Sayrafa trading volume denominated in USD.

## effective_days
Number of calendar days during which the published rate remained effective until the next available publication date.
